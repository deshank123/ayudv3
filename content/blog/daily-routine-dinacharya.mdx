# Daily Routine (Dinacharya)

Dinacharya is the Ayurvedic practice of following a daily routine aligned with nature's rhythms. This practice helps maintain balance in body, mind, and consciousness.

## Morning Routine (4:00 AM - 10:00 AM)
1. Wake up before sunrise (Brahma Muhurta)
2. Clean tongue and brush teeth
3. Drink warm water
4. Practice meditation and yoga
5. Exercise according to constitution
6. Light breakfast

## Mid-Day Routine (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)
1. Main meal of the day
2. Most productive work
3. Stay hydrated
4. Take short walks

## Evening Routine (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
1. Light physical activity
2. Creative work
3. Light snack if needed
4. Begin winding down

## Night Routine (6:00 PM - 10:00 PM)
1. Light dinner before sunset
2. Gentle walking
3. Relaxing activities
4. Early bedtime

Following this routine helps establish healthy habits and promotes natural healing and rejuvenation.
